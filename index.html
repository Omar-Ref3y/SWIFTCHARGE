Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to fully charge the SwiftCharge?

With the included 100W USB-C charger, SwiftCharge can be fully recharged in just 1.5 hours, which is 3x faster than conventional power banks of similar capacity.

Can SwiftCharge charge my laptop?

Yes! SwiftCharge delivers up to 100W of power through USB-C Power Delivery, which is sufficient to charge most laptops including MacBooks, Dell XPS, and other USB-C compatible laptops.

Is SwiftCharge safe to take on airplanes?

Absolutely. SwiftCharge is fully compliant with TSA and international air travel regulations for lithium-ion batteries in carry-on luggage (under 100Wh).

SwiftCharge features 3 output ports (2 USB-C and 1 USB-A), allowing you to charge up to 3 devices simultaneously while intelligently distributing power for optimal charging speeds.
